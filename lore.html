Roman Pantheon

In the beginning, Bahamut, the oldest of the gods, took eleven scales from his side and created the eleven gods we know today. After the gods continued his work in creating reality, he breathed his fire upon lowly serpents and created the dragons that inhabit our realm. Because he removed his scales, Bahamut will forever be at risk of death, so he seldom visits our realm.

Aljin Pantheon

In the beginning, Bahamut flew through the heavens, breathing his fire and lighting the stars one by one. He grew lonely, though, and decided to create our realm next. From the water, he formed the first dragons, Zehir, Ioun, Corellon, Kord, and Melora. They helped Bahamut imagine all that is and bring it into being. Bahamut declared that the world should remain wild and chaotic to balance the natural order of death brought by Zehir.
