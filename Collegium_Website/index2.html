Welcome to Collegium!

What if the Roman Empire never fell and, instead of becoming more secular, became more attuned with the arcane? With the magic and monsters of the D&D world mixed with the mythology and deities of Rome, who knows what will happen? You could fight skeleton gladiators in the arena for the Glory of the Emperor, complete epic quests for the Guild, or build an empire of your own to conquer Rome. Anything is possible in Collegium!
